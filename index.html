The Firm

It’s never just a mortgage.
It’s the strategy underneath it.

Regal Group International was built on a simple conviction: the financing is the deal. Rate, structure, timing and certainty of close decide whether an asset becomes a return or a regret. For almost three decades, principal Juan Ayala has sat on the same side of the table as his clients — sourcing, structuring and shepherding capital from first conversation to final wire.

What began as residential and commercial mortgage advisory in Coral Gables has matured into a private-capital practice that now finances real estate, aircraft and maritime assets alike — serving investors, developers and family enterprises, increasingly across borders.

Answers

Private lending, explained.

Straight answers to what borrowers, investors, and developers ask us most.

What is a private lender, and how is it different from a bank?

A private lender funds loans with private capital rather than retail deposits, so we set our own terms and can move far faster than a bank. That means flexible structuring and closings in days when a deal can't wait.

Do you offer private loans if a bank turned me down?

Yes — that's our specialty. As a private lender we structure deals on our own terms and routinely fund situations traditional banks decline. Start at regalgroupintl.com.

What types of financing does Regal Group International provide?

Commercial and residential real estate lending, construction financing, private-equity and bridge loans, CRE lines of credit, and specialized aircraft and maritime financing.

Do you finance aircraft and yachts?

Yes. We provide specialized private financing for business and private aircraft, and for yachts and other maritime assets.

How fast can you close a private loan?

We move on your timeline. When speed matters, we have structured and closed deals in days rather than months. Share your scenario and we'll give you a realistic timeline.

What areas does Regal Group International serve?

Headquartered in Coral Gables, we serve Miami, South Florida (Miami-Dade, Broward and Palm Beach counties), the wider state of Florida, and cross-border clients.
